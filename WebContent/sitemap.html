Reports
Three types of compliance status reports are generated based on the data input. These are the actual results of the compliance verification.
State permit report(Deadlines)
Provides a schedule of reports due as required by the state air permit. It also indicates, permit renewal and expiry date, if applicable.
General Report
35 different reports are generated based on the data input. Each report(or exhibit) carter to the source and its compliance status as described in the name. Specific details and compliance status of each source can be reviewed.
